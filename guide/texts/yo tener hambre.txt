When I was first learning Spanish I said to the Hostess "Yo tener hambre". She laughed. Then she corrected me. She said "yo tengo hambre". But then she immediately went to the kitchen and began to prepare something for me to eat. The point is, communications happened there even though I spoke the phrase badly. The point is that it doesn't matter if you say something wrong. What matters is that you can make yourself understood and can understand. That is communication. So don't worry about getting everything right. You will gradually refine that as you go. Feel free to make mistakes.
